About Gleam Cleaning Solutions

Located at Richmond House, Mfangano Street, Gleam Cleaning Solutions is a Nairobi-based full-service cleaning company providing residential, commercial and vehicle cleaning across Nairobi and surrounding areas. Our trained teams use eco-friendly products and professional equipment to deliver consistent, reliable results.

Our Mission

To transform spaces through professional cleaning that prioritizes client health, safety and the environment.

Values

  • Professionalism
  • Reliability
  • Eco-safety
  • Customer focus

Team & Equipment

Our technicians are trained in standard safety and cleaning protocols. We use commercial-grade vacuums, steam extractors, pressure washers and safe detergents to get lasting results.
